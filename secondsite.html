Ratatouille
The main dish. This is not only a common dish in France, it is THEE dish that made Ego give five stars back to Gusteau's restaurant. It brought tears to his eyes, it'll bring tears to yours.
Omelette Du Fromage
This is a cheese omlette with stolen spices from the neighbor's garden. The thievery gives the omlette a kick. This is not only a popular and customizable breakfast option, it is what help strengthen Linguini and Remy's friendship
Potato and Leek Soup
This is the soup that started it all. Without Remy being utterly revolted by Linguini's improvise spice mix, Remy would've never had his talents recognized. Now he is known throughout the world as "Little Chef"
SweetBreads Ala Gusteau
The dish that almost got Linguini slapped. This dish is what taught us that we should not follow our idols step by step. Celebrate others accomplishments but don't let our creativity be caged by their work Many recipes on this site!
